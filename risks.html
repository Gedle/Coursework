Misalignment between a web developer and a client: that is a website may be beautiful, with a great user experience,
but if it overlooks, or does not 1. adequately represent how the client wants the website to look, 2. what
information they want their target audience, to take away from the website, along with the business processes,
the website is very likely to fail.

Therefore a project should I believe not just rely on web developers in each stage of producing a website.

There should be a crossover of skills and expertise, not just technical/IT, involved in the whole process.

Planning is important so as to determine budget, scope and time limits of the project.
